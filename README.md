## 📖 Overview

This research analyzes the **2023–2024 Red Sea Crisis** and its **systemic disruption of global trade routes**, with a focus on:

- 🌐 Geopolitical risk monitoring  
- 📊 Economic intelligence & decision-making  
- 🚢 Maritime logistics disruption  
- 📦 Supply chain resilience strategies

## 📊 Research Highlights

### 🕒 Timeline

- **Late 2023** → Houthi attacks begin  
- **Q1–Q2 2024** → Peak disruption  
- **2024+** → Structural supply chain shifts  

---

### 📈 Critical Signals

- 📊 Freight rates surged **+363%**  
- ⚠️ Insurance premiums rose *before* attacks  
- 🏭 Tesla Berlin halted production (**2 weeks**)  

---

### 🏢 Strategic Responses

- **IKEA** → Buffer stock + dual sourcing  
- **Walmart** → US West Coast rerouting + rail  
- **EU Automakers** → Air freight (+500% cost)  
- **Maersk** → Suspension → escorted resumption
